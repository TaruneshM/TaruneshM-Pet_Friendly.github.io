Our Mission

Pet-Friendly City began when a group of college students noticed the struggles of street animals in their neighborhood. What started as a small feeding initiative has grown into a city-wide movement.

We believe that compassion for animals makes us better humans. Our programs focus on sustainable solutions that benefit both animals and communities.

  • 5,000+ animals fed monthly
  • 1,200+ rescues to date
  • 300+ successful adoptions
  • 15 community partnerships
